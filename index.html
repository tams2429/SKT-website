About me

I am an aspiring developer with a particular interest towards the Front-End aspect. With regards to this, I have focused primarily on self-learning HTML, CSS and Javascript through the use of online resources including FreeCodeCamp, Codewars and Youtube.

This is my first website and has been created to support my application to a coding bootcamp, from a combination of HTML, CSS and Vanilla Javascript.

For the past 4 years, up until 6 months ago, I went straight from a Civil Engineering degree in university to working in the industry. However, I couldn’t shake off a lack of fulfilment in the job I was doing.

Having dabbled in coding from time to time outside of work and experiencing the euphoria that comes with a eureka moment after struggling through a problem for so long, I wish to start anew in my career, with coding, where I feel a sense of satisfaction in the work I do.

However, as work began to pick up and my time to code became more limited, I decided to fully commit to the cause and quit my job, which takes me to where I am now. Ready and looking for any opportunity that is out there.

As a kid I always liked to build things...

As a result, I pursued the path of an Engineer all throughout my school life...

To the point where I had achieved my goal and worked as a Civil Engineer for a few years...

On the side, I enjoy exploring my creative side by making small videos...

Ultimately, I wanted to pursue web development to combine my passion for creativity with building things...

Why am I applying to F&C?

Through the many struggles that I have encountered since learning to code, I know all too well how difficult it can be to keep going on your own, especially when a simple google search/stack overflow seems to always not provide the answer simple enough for someone starting out to understand. As the saying goes, ‘Two minds are always better than One’, or in the case of F&C, a whole class cohort can be my best friend in developing/learning to code.

In addition to providing me with a constructive learning environment, I hope that F&C will be able to provide me with the solid foundation that I need in order to get my foot in the door to a highly competitive industry.

As someone who is also lacking in experience, the idea of having a project for a real, external client is also something I am very keen about. One which, I know that F&C are able to provide along with tools outside of coding including design and entrepreneurship.
